Adding a link to a glossary entry

Links to glossary entries are inserted and displayed differently from other types of links.

You can insert a link on a word which your users may find unfamiliar. When clicking the link, a pop-up containing the definition of the word opens. 

Glossary definitions are typically one to two sentences long. If the topic is too complex for a short format, we recommend you use the Concepts strategy instead.

To insert a link to a glossary entry:

  1. Highlight the word you want to put the link on.
  2. Click the Insert Glossary Term icon in the text editor.



    The following window opens:



    By default, the word you highlighted is preselected. You can change it.
  3. Click Process search.
    Edc searches through the Glossary strategy to find all potential matches. Here, only one exists.



    This result corresponds to an entry in the Glossary strategy:



    If no result is found, you may need to create a new Glossary entry.
  4. Click OK.
    The link is inserted.
